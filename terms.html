About GetPrivacyScores

GetPrivacyScores (getprivacyscores.com) is a collaborative, community-powered privacy ranking platform. We analyze the Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy of websites around the world, providing users with a transparent trust score from 0 to 10.

Our rankings are generated through the Privacy Check browser extension, which uses artificial intelligence to evaluate legal documents. All data submitted to our ranking database is anonymous — no personal information about the users who perform analyses is collected or stored.

💡 GetPrivacyScores is currently free for all users. We are committed to maintaining this service as a public resource for digital privacy awareness.

03

How the Service Works

The Privacy Check Extension

The Privacy Check extension is a browser tool that automatically locates and analyzes the legal documents of websites you visit. When you trigger an analysis, the extension:

  • Identifies links to Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy pages on the current site
  • Fetches and extracts the text content of those documents
  • Sends the text to our AI-powered backend for analysis
  • Returns a score from 0–10 for each document category
  • Anonymously submits the result to the GetPrivacyScores ranking database

The Ranking Database

Each analysis contributes to a running weighted average for the analyzed website. The final ranking score shown on GetPrivacyScores is the average of all three category scores (Terms, Privacy, Cookies). Sites with missing documents receive a score of 0 for that category.

AI-Generated Scores

Scores are generated by an AI model and are intended as informational guidance only. They do not constitute legal advice and should not be relied upon as a definitive legal assessment of any website's practices.

⚠️ Privacy scores are AI-generated estimates and may not reflect all aspects of a website's actual data practices. Always read legal documents yourself for important decisions.
