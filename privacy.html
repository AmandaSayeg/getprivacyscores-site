01

Who We Are

GetPrivacyScores (getprivacyscores.com) is a collaborative privacy ranking platform. We analyze the legal documents of websites and make the results publicly available so users can make informed decisions about their digital privacy.

Our service includes this website and the Privacy Check browser extension, available on the Chrome Web Store.

02

What We Collect

We designed GetPrivacyScores with a minimal data footprint. Here is a complete overview of what we collect — and what we deliberately do not collect:

Data type Collected? Details
Name or email address Never No accounts or sign-ups required
IP address Never stored May pass through servers transiently but is not logged or retained
Browsing history Never The extension does not track which sites you visit
Personal identifiers Never No cookies, fingerprinting, or device identifiers
Anonymous site analysis results Yes — anonymously When you run an analysis, the domain name and AI-generated score are stored. No user identity is attached.
Anonymous extension usage ID Yes — locally only A random ID stored locally in the extension to prevent duplicate submissions. Never sent to our servers with any identifying info.
03

The Privacy Check Extension

The Privacy Check extension runs entirely on your device and in our secure backend. Here is exactly what happens when you run an analysis:

  1. The extension detects legal document links on the current website
  2. It fetches the content of those documents (Terms, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy)
  3. The text is sent to our backend API for AI analysis — no personal data is included
  4. The backend returns scores and analysis results to your browser
  5. The domain name and scores are anonymously recorded in our ranking database

What the extension does NOT do

  • Does not read your form inputs, passwords, or personal data on any page
  • Does not track which websites you visit unless you explicitly trigger an analysis
  • Does not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising
  • Does not run in the background without your interaction

✅ The extension only activates when you click on it. It does not passively monitor your browsing.

04

How We Use Data

The anonymous analysis data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Build and maintain the public privacy ranking on GetPrivacyScores
  • Calculate weighted average scores per website over time
  • Improve the accuracy and consistency of our AI analysis

We do not use any data for advertising, marketing profiling, or sale to third parties.

05

Data Sharing

We share data with the following service providers, strictly for operational purposes:

Anthropic (AI Analysis)

Legal document text is sent to Anthropic's API for AI-powered analysis. Anthropic processes this text to generate privacy scores. No personal user data is included. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

Supabase (Database)

Our ranking database is hosted on Supabase, deployed in the US East region. Only anonymous domain names and scores are stored. Supabase's practices are covered by their Privacy Policy.

Vercel (Hosting)

This website and our backend API are hosted on Vercel. Standard server logs may be retained by Vercel per their Privacy Policy.

⚠️ We never sell data to third parties, data brokers, or advertisers. Ever.

06

Data Retention

Anonymous ranking data (domain names and scores) is retained indefinitely as it forms the core of our public database. This data contains no personal information.

The anonymous local extension ID stored in your browser can be cleared at any time by removing the Privacy Check extension or clearing your browser's extension storage.

07

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) are not applicable — there is simply no personal data about you to access or delete.

However, you have the following controls:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection
  • Clear extension storage in your browser to remove the local anonymous ID
  • Contact us if you have any questions about data associated with a specific domain you submitted

If you are located in the European Union, you may have additional rights under the GDPR. If you are located in California, you may have additional rights under the CCPA. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

08

Security

We implement the following security measures to protect our systems and the data we hold:

  • All communications between the extension, our API, and the database use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • API keys and service credentials are stored as environment variables, never in source code
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) is enabled on our database, restricting write access to our backend only
  • The public ranking data is read-only for all visitors

While we implement these protections, no system can guarantee 100% security. We encourage you to report any security concerns to security@getprivacyscores.com.

09

Children's Privacy

If you believe a child has submitted information through our service, please contact us and we will address it promptly.
